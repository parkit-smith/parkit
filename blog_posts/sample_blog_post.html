Sample Title 2024

Parkit at Smith College is an initiative focused on creating accessible, community-driven green spaces through tactical urbanism. The project fosters collaboration between students, faculty, and the local community to design and implement parks that promote sustainability and public engagement. With a blend of research, events, and hands-on projects, Parkit aims to enrich urban spaces while addressing environmental concerns. This effort encourages innovative approaches to urban planning and highlights the importance of integrating nature into daily life.

The Parkit website at Smith College introduces their innovative project focused on bringing parks to people through tactical urbanism. Parkit highlights sustainability, community, and accessible public spaces, with events, research, and a dedicated team driving the initiative. Their work is geared towards urban engagement and environmental stewardship, with ongoing projects and resources shared with the wider Smith community.
